Ingredients

  • 1 shot Rum
  • 1/2 shot Dry Vermouth
  • 1/2 shot Cognac
  • 1/2 shot Gin
  • 1/4 shot Fresh Lime Juice
  • 1/4 shot Sugar Syrup
  • 1/2 shot Water

Instructions

Shake together all the ingredients and strain into a cold glass.

Alcoholic

Yes

Serve

Martini Glass
